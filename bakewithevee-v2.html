About

BakeWithEvee started in 2022. No storefront, no walk-ins — just weekly pre-order batches made from scratch at home.

Everything is made with Japanese-imported flour. The quality is genuinely different from what you get locally. Better protein structure, finer milling — it shows up in the crust and the crumb.

The menu changes slightly every week. Some weeks it's more sourdough-heavy, some weeks there are specials. We announce what's available every Wednesday on Instagram Stories, and orders open every Sunday.

From flour, water and salt — through patience, time, skill and love — bring it all together. BakeWithEvee is born.
