About

BakeWithEvee started in 2022. No storefront, no walk-ins. Just weekly pre-order batches made from scratch at home in Miri.

Everything is made with Japanese-imported flour. The quality is different from what you get locally — better protein structure, finer milling. It shows in the crust and the crumb.

The menu changes slightly every week. We announce what's available every Wednesday on Instagram Stories. Orders open every Sunday.

From flour, water and salt — through patience, time, skill and love — bring it all together. BakeWithEvee is born.
